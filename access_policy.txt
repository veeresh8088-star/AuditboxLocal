Company Access Control Policy
Version: 1.0
Scope: All employees and systems

1. Access Control:
- Access to company systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- Access rights must be granted based on the principle of least privilege and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- Periodic access reviews must be conducted every quarter to ensure authorization validity and remove orphaned accounts.
- Access must be revoked immediately upon termination of employment.

2. Authentication and MFA:
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) must be enforced for all external access via VPN and cloud environments.
- Strong password complexity and rotation policies are enforced: minimum 12 characters, including upper, lower, numbers, and symbols. Passwords must be rotated every 90 days.

3. Incident Management:
- All employees must report security incidents and suspicious events immediately to the security team.
- The incident response team will assess and triage all events to contain and remediate them.

4. Backups and Business Continuity:
- Automated daily backups must be taken and stored securely.
- Disaster Recovery (DR) and backup restoration testing must be performed annually to verify RTO and RPO.
